Shoyu Chicken

  • 2 pounds chicken thighs
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1" ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup shoyu (soy sauce)
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon green onions, sliced

Brown chicken thighs with garlic and ginger. Add shoyu and sugar and simmer for 20 minutes on medium heat. Add green onions just before serving.

Serve with rice and vegetables.
