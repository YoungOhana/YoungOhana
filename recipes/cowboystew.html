Cowboy Stew

  • 1 1/4 pounds stew meat, cut in 1" cubes
  • 4 medium unpeeled potatoes, cut in 1" cubes
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 can (28 oz) barbecue flavored baked beans

Combine ingredients in crockpot. Cook for 8-10 hours, or until meat is tender.
