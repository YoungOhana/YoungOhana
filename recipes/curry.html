Chicken Curry

  • 1 box House Vermont Curry Sauce Mix
  • 1 lb Chicken thighs cubed
  • 1 onion cubed
  • 3 potatoes cubed
  • 1 carrot sliced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  1. Saute ingredients: Preheat oil in a pot, add bite-size pieces of meat and vegetables, and saut√© well or until the onions are lightly browned
  2. Add water and simmer: Add about 3 1/2 cups water and simmer until ingredients soften (about 15 minutes).
  3. Add curry sauce mix: Break apart curry sauce mix into the pot and stir until dissolved.
  4. Simmer: Simmer once more, stirring occasionally (about 10 minutes) or until curry thickens.
  5. Optional add honey: Add it in Step 2, and after bringing the curry to a boil, simmer at least 20 minutes.
  6. Note: May replace chicken with other meat.
