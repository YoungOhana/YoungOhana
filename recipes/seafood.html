Garlic Butter Mahimahi

  • 1 lb mahimahi fillet
  • 1 garlic clove, sliced
  • 1 tsp butter or margarine

Oil medium pot. Add mahimahi fillet to pot, skin side down. Sprinkle garlic and butter on top. Cook for 20 minutes on medium-low heat or until flaky.
