Chip N Dip

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • Heinz® Ketchup
  • Tortilla Chips
  • Salsa
  • Kraft® Sharp Cheddar Cheese, grated or cubed

Brown ground beef with onion and garlic. Add ketchup to taste. Serve with chips, salsa, and Cheddar Cheese.
