Young Ohana Recipes - Beef

Casserole

  • 1 package elbow macaroni
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 can Hunt's® Diced Tomatoes
  • 1 can Hunt's® Tomato Sauce

Cook macaroni according to package directions.

Brown ground beef with onion and garlic. Add diced tomatoes and tomato sauce and macaroni and heat through or bake in the oven for 20 minutes at 325 degrees.

Serve with garlic bread or French bread.

Chili

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 package Carroll Shelby's® Chili Kit
  • 2 cans dark red kidney beans, drained
  • 1 can Hunt's® Diced Tomatoes
  • 1 can Hunt's® Tomato Sauce

Brown ground beef with onion and garlic. Add flavor packets from kit leaving out flour packet. Add dark red kidney beans, diced tomatoes, and tomato sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.

Serve over rice with your favorite fixings such as grated cheese or diced onions.

Chip N Dip

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • ketchup
  • tortilla chips
  • salsa
  • sharp cheddar cheese, grated

Brown ground beef with onion and garlic. Add ketchup to taste. Serve with chips, salsa, and grated cheese.

Cottage Pie

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can Campbell's® Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 1 package frozen green beans or peas
  • mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup cheddar or parmesan cheese

Brown ground beef with onion and garlic. Add mushroom soup and green beans and mix. Transfer to 9" x 12" baking dish. Spread mashed potatoes on top. Spread cheese on top of potatoes. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Serve with tossed salad and French bread.

Ground Beef Stew

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 carrot, peeled and chopped
  • 1 potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 can Hunt's® Diced Tomatoes
  • 1 can Hunt's® Tomato Sauce

Brown ground beef with onion and garlic. Add carrots and potatoes and simmer until tender. Add diced tomatoes and tomato sauce and simmer until hot.

Serve over rice.
