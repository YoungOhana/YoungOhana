Cottage Pie

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can Campbell'sÂ® Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 1 package frozen green beans
  • mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup cheddar or parmesan cheese

Brown ground beef with onion and garlic. Add mushroom soup and green beans and mix. Transfer to 9" x 12" baking dish. Spread mashed potatoes on top. Spread cheese on top of potatoes. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Serve with tossed salad and French bread.
