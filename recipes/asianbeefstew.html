Asian Beef Stew

  • 2 pounds potatoes, peeled and sliced 1/2 inch thick
  • 1 pound carrots, washed and chopped 1 inch long
  • 1 onion, sliced thin
  • 3 pounds stew meat
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 3 cups low sodium beef broth
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Asian chili paste
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch

Layer potatoes, carrots, onion, and beef in a 5 to 6-quart slow cooker. Whisk together remaining ingredients and add to slow cooker. Cover slow cooker and cook on high heat 5 hours.

Serve with hot rice.
